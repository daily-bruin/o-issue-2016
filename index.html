Orientation Issue 2016

For all the new Bruins hoping to learn some of the ins and outs of life at UCLA before heading to Westwood, look no further. We've got answers to a wide range of questions and insight into various topics, including: Where are the best spots across Los Angeles to release your inner hipster? Who are some of the important athletes to know before the fall season? Why will the quarter system often force you to have midterms for at least six weeks straight?

As one of the lucky individuals who gets to attend UCLA in the fall, you shouldn't just wander into your short time in college - you should hit the ground running. That's where we come in. No propaganda, no nonsense, just the information you need to make your time as a Bruin the best it can be.
